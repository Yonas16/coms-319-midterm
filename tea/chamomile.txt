Chamomile tea is one of the more popular teas, as it is well know for its soothing effects. It has a positive impact on human ailments such as gastrointestinal disturbances including flatulence, indigestion, diarrhea, anorexia, motion sickness, nausea, and vomiting. Chamomile tea is best to drink right before going to sleep, as its soothing effects are more noticeable then.
