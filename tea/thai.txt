Thai tea is a great tea to have iced. It is a sweet, creamy tea commonly enjoyed in Thailand, often served iced. It is made from strong brewed black tea, typically combined with spices such as star anise, tamarind, and sometimes orange blossom water. The tea is then sweetened with sugar and condensed milk, which gives it a rich, creamy texture. Its is known for its orange color which often times comes from food coloring. Best over ice with evaporated milk or half and half.
